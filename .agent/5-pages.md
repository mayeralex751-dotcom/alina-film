# agent/5-pages.md — Page Structure "ReneeInspiredFilm"

Required pages:

1. / (Home)
   - Hero section: large film photo + title + short bio
   - Recent work gallery (6–8 photos)
   - About teaser + link
   - Contact CTA

2. /about
   - Bio text
   - Photo of photographer
   - Film cameras used
   - Philosophy / style

3. /blog
   - List of posts (title, date, excerpt, featured image)
   - Pagination
   - Categories/tags sidebar (optional)

4. /blog/[slug]
   - Full post with images
   - Date, reading time
   - Share buttons

5. /contact
   - Contact form (name, email, message, optional file upload)
   - Success message
   - Alternative contact (email, Instagram)

Optional:
- /gallery (dedicated portfolio)
- /films (cameras & film stock used)
- /faq

Navigation:
- Home
- About
- Blog
- Contact

Footer:
- Copyright
- Social links (Instagram, etc.)
- Privacy policy link (later)
